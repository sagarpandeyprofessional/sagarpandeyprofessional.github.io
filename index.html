Motem Flumen Construction and Robotics
Co-Founder

Montem Flumen Construction and Robotics, co-founded with a vision for innovation, stands at the forefront of revolutionizing the construction industry. Our primary objective is to automate construction processes through the integration of cutting-edge drone and robot technologies. Traditional construction methods often involve time-intensive processes, require substantial manpower, and pose risks to workers in hazardous conditions. Our groundbreaking technology addresses these challenges by prioritizing safety and expediting construction timelines. Particularly beneficial for countries with lower populations, our solutions offer a transformative approach, minimizing reliance on extensive workforces and enhancing overall efficiency in construction projects. Montem Flumen is dedicated to paving the way for a safer, faster, and more technologically advanced future in the construction sector. As Co-Founder of MF Construction & Robotics, I lead a pioneering startup dedicated to revolutionizing construction through robotics. I drive strategic planning and development, guide decision-making processes, and assemble dynamic teams. My multifaceted role ensures alignment with objectives and fosters innovation for sustainable growth.
